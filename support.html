Common questions

"I lost my data when I deleted the app."

All my apps are local-first by default — when you delete the app, the data goes with it. Some apps have export options in Settings; check before deleting if you want to preserve anything.

"Can I sync between my iPhone and iPad?"

For most apps, the answer is "not yet" — local-first means single-device by default. If a specific app supports iCloud sync, it will say so in its Settings.

"Will my data ever be sold or shared?"

No. See my privacy policy.

"I bought a paid app and don't want it anymore."

App Store refund requests go through Apple, not me: https://reportaproblem.apple.com

"I'd like to suggest a feature."

Please email me — I'm always open to ideas. Solo dev, so I can't promise everything ships, but I read everything.
