TELLS — Privacy Policy

Effective: March 28, 2026

Overview

TELLS is a single-player game that runs entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

TELLS collects no data. The app does not require an internet connection, does not use analytics services, does not track your activity, and does not contain advertising.

Third-Party Services

TELLS uses no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, ad networks, or cloud services. All game data (progress, scores, settings) is stored locally on your device and is not accessible to us or anyone else.

Microphone & Camera

TELLS does not access your microphone, camera, photos, contacts, location, or any other device sensors or personal data.

Children

TELLS does not collect data from anyone, including children. The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
