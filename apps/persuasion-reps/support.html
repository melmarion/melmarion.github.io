Common questions

"I lost my data when I deleted the app."

Persuasion Reps is local-first — when you delete the app, its data goes with it. If it has an export option it's in Settings; export before deleting if you want to keep anything.

"Can I sync between my iPhone and iPad?"

Local-first means single-device by default. If Persuasion Reps supports iCloud sync, it will say so in its Settings.

"Will my data ever be sold or shared?"

No. See the privacy policy.

"I bought it and want a refund."

App Store refunds go through Apple, not me: reportaproblem.apple.com

"I'd like to suggest a feature."
